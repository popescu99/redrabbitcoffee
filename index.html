About Us

A small room built around a good cup of coffee.

At Red Rabbit Specialty Coffee, every cup is prepared with attention to detail and respect for the craft of specialty coffee. We source thoughtfully, brew deliberately, and treat hospitality as seriously as we treat the espresso.

Our mission is simple: create a space where great coffee, friendly people, and memorable moments come together — whether you're here for a quiet hour with a book or an afternoon of work with friends.

Our Craft

From bean to cup, the rabbit's path.

Specialty coffee is a chain of small decisions. We follow it carefully, one hop at a time, so the cup in your hands tastes the way it should.

The Bean

Seasonal lots, chosen for clarity and character, roasted to highlight origin rather than mask it.

The Grind

Dialled in by hand for every method, because filter, espresso, and pour-over all ask for something different.

The Brew

Timed, weighed, and tasted before it ever reaches your table — consistency is the quiet part of hospitality.

The Cup

Served by people who actually want to talk to you about it — or leave you be, whichever you need.

Our coffee, made a few ways.

A focused range across classic espresso drinks, filter coffee, and the occasional seasonal experiment.

Espresso

Concentrated, balanced, and the base for everything we pour.

Cappuccino

Equal parts espresso, milk, and foam — texture done properly.

Flat White

Velvety microfoam over a double ristretto. Quietly our favorite.

Latte

Mellow and milk-forward, with room for a flavour of the week.

Filter Coffee

Clean, bright, single-origin pour-over — for tasting the bean itself.

Seasonal Specials

Short-run drinks built around what's in season — ask what's on.

Alternative Brewing

V60, AeroPress, and other methods for the curious drinker.

For Every Mood

Decaf, oat, and lighter options are always quietly available.
